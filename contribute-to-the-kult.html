Contribute to the Kult

Kult of the Cyber Witch accepts all submissions from all individuals, however, focuses on people of marginalised genders and sexes experiences within the Oceania region. All published contributors will receive an honorarium, or can opt to donate this back to zine.

We are looking for a variety of articles from beginner to advance on technical topics such as (but not limited to): - Physical Security, Lockport, Tamper Evident and/or Red-Teaming - Blue-Teaming, Purple-Teaming, Defending and Defensive Security - Using Open-Source Technology to secure/monitor your environments, solutions - Accessibility and Security

We are also interested in hearing about your experiences in the industry and social issues currently faced in the Oceania region, such as mental health or how government policies affect end-users and/or businesses as well as works of fiction themed around information security and privacy, of course there are also culture submissions like how to be the best dressed cyber witch at the summer solstice.

We also love art-based or technical art based submissions! Due to limitations with printing costs only the front and back cover are done in full cover - for more details feel free to contact us at callforideas (at) kultuofthecyberwitch (dot) org.

After Submitting

We start reviewing submissions as soon as the Call for Contributors is closed. Depending on other factors, it may take 4 to 5 weeks to contact everyone who submitted, if you do submit please ensure the email address you use is up-to date, if you change your email address after submitting please contact us.

If we accept your submission, we will contact you with further information about payment, and a short contract to sign, contract will allow you to retain the copyright to your work as well as information about proceeding with the submission.

We ask that if your submission is accepted you be available to work with the editors to finalise your contribution, we understand life can get the best of us if something last minute comes up please let us know as soon as possible and we can arrange something unique to your circumstances.

After you submit your draft, we will begin to work with you to resolve any questions about content, structure, spelling and grammar. Our goal is to ensure you are heard loud and clear and that any technical details are accessible to the level you are aiming at.

When everyone is happy with the article we will confirm placement and layout. You will also be able to provide a short bio about yourself!
