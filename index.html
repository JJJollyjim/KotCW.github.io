Kult of the Cyber Witch is a holistic information security and culture zine that is super SpooOoOooOOopy.

Our publication centers on people of marginalised genders and sexes experiences within the Oceania region. We welcome all readers and contributors, whether you have been doing information security ~things~ for years, are just starting out, are a curious weekend dabbler, or self-taught part-timer. We cover a variety of topics including social and technical problems we face, as well as works of fiction and things that are just generally witchy.

Behold the wonder that is contributing to Kult of the Cyber Witch.
