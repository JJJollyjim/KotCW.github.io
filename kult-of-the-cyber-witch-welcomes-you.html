Kult of the Cyber Witch Welcomes You

Kult of the Cyber Witch is a holistic information security and culture zine that centres on people of marginalised genders/sexes experiences and voices within the Oceania region.

We welcome all readers and contributors, whether you have been in the Information Security industry for years, are just starting out, are a curious weekend dabbler, or self-taught part-timer.

We aim to cover a variety of topics including social and technical problems we face, as well as works of fiction.

If following questions call to you, you too might be a Cyber Witch and will enjoy being part of the community!

  • Do you find yourself invoking the spirits via Console?
  • Do you use pipe /dev/null as a banishing spell?
  • When a spell or ritual isn't working right, do you conjure the likes of Google, DuckDuckGo or Stack Overflow?
  • Does your smart phone double up as a candle used during spells and rituals?
  • Is your familiar a mouse?
  • Do you use Snort or Wireshark for divination?
  • Do you find yourself reviewing CVEs and the latest security issues as part of your regular warding process?
  • Do you find yourself accessing the astral plane via Chrome, Brave, or Firefox?
  • Do you consecrate your computer regularly with security patches and updates?
  • Do you carve, etch, or mark your devices with protective sigils?
